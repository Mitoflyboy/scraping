Description

"BELL HILL" is a private retreat, nestled on a hill overlooking the stunning Orange landscape and the 2000 acre working farm. It is conveniently located only 10 mins drive on tar road from Orange and its well known restaurants, wineries, shops and golf courses.

The house has 2 large living areas, one formal and the other informal. Each living room has a cosy fireplace to warm yourself with. There's a large dining table in each of these rooms so you're able to enjoy your meals in either of the cosy rooms.

Magnificent views of the Orange countryside are found from every room of the house. All rooms and bedrooms have French doors opening onto the wrap around verandah of the house.

Enjoy cooking in the fully equipped gourmet kitchen or take in the spectacular sunsets from the outdoor entertaining area.....so much choice, so relax and enjoy

All rates include Sheridan linen & towels for the guests. Complimentary soaps, shampoos and conditioners are also included.

BEDROOM CONFIGURATION

4 LARGE BEDROOMS

master suite with a king bed

two rooms with a queen bed in each

fourth room holds one comfortable set of bunks, one single bed and two trundles sleeps 5.

A stunning large carrera marble bathroom is located off the main hallway with a free standing bath, shower and toilet.

Master suite has its own marble ensuite with shower and toilet.

All bedrooms have large built ins and the master suite is generously proportioned with its own walk in robe.

All bedrooms have their own French doors leading onto a covered verandah which takes in views of the garden and countryside.

NO PETS ALLOWED

Due to the fact that Bell Hill is a working property there are strictly no pets allowed.
